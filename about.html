Our Story

Malaysia's Trusted Supply Partner

Mediniaga Plus Sdn Bhd is a well-established Malaysian distributor and manufacturer of medical devices, consumables, and hygiene solutions. With over a decade of experience in the healthcare and supply chain industry, we have built strong, exclusive partnerships with leading global brands — enabling us to provide comprehensive and innovative solutions across various sectors.

Headquartered in Kuala Lumpur, we maintain physical offices and warehousing facilities throughout Malaysia. Our distribution network serves public and private hospitals, laboratories, research centers, universities, clinics, nursing homes, and day surgery centers.
