Use of Personal Protective Equipment During the COVID-19 Pandemic

March 31, 2025 · Mediniaga Team

The COVID-19 pandemic fundamentally changed how healthcare facilities approach personal protective equipment (PPE). What was once a standard consideration became the single most critical factor in patient and staff safety.

Key Takeaways

  • Global PPE demand increased by over 400% during peak pandemic periods
  • Disposable gloves became the most consumed PPE item worldwide
  • Malaysia emerged as a key manufacturing hub for medical gloves
  • Proper donning and doffing procedures became mandatory training

PPE in the Post-Pandemic Era

Even as the pandemic recedes, the baseline consumption of PPE remains significantly elevated. Healthcare facilities now maintain higher stock levels, and infection control protocols have been permanently strengthened. This has created sustained demand for examination gloves, isolation gowns, face masks, and biohazard disposal supplies.

For procurement managers, this means reliable supply chains and consistent quality have become even more important than pre-pandemic levels. Mediniaga continues to support Malaysian healthcare facilities with certified PPE products at competitive bulk pricing.
